About Me

Building state-of-the-art, easy to use, user-friendly websites and applications is truly a passion of mine. My markup is always semantic, accessible and responsive. I love CSS, and can build almost any design. Check out my works on Github .

Can't fully call myself a full fledged UI/UX designer but I dabble with creating clean and user-friendly UI/UX designs with Figma, Photoshop and Adobe XD for web and mobile platforms; and I have some pretty good works to show for it on Behance .
